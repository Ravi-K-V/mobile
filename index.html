🎤 Voice Dictation Tips for Medical Fields

🎨 Mic Button Color Guide:

Gray = Off/Idle - Click to start recording
Green (pulsing) = Recording - Speak clearly, click again to stop
Red (brief) = Stopping - Processing your speech
Orange = Error - Check microphone permissions

💡 Tips: Recording continues until you click again or after 5 seconds of silence. Text is added to the field as you speak. Multiple recordings append to existing text.

Say this → Get this:

  • "minus two point five" → -2.5
  • "plus one point seven five" → +1.75
  • "six over six" → 6/6
  • "twenty over twenty" → 20/20
  • "at ninety degrees" → @90°

Supported conversions:

  • "minus" or "negative" → -
  • "plus" or "positive" → +
  • "point" → . (decimal)
  • "over" → / (fraction)
  • Number words → digits (e.g., "six" → 6)
